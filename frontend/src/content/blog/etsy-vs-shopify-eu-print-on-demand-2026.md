The Etsy vs. Shopify question for EU print-on-demand comes down to one number: how many sales are you generating per month, and where is that traffic coming from?

At low volume, Etsy's built-in marketplace traffic makes it the correct choice despite higher transaction fees. At higher volume with an established audience, Shopify's lower fee structure unlocks margin that Etsy's percentage-based costs consume. The crossover point is calculable.

## Fee Structure Comparison (2026)

**Etsy EU seller fees:**
- Listing fee: €0.18 per listing, renewed every 4 months
- Transaction fee: 6.5% of sale price (including shipping)
- Payment processing: 4% + €0.30 (EU cards, varies slightly by country)
- Etsy Ads: optional, typically 15–25% of revenue for competitive categories
- EU VAT: collected and remitted by Etsy for B2C sales under OSS threshold

**Shopify EU seller fees (Basic plan at €27/month):**
- Monthly subscription: €27/month (Basic) or €79/month (Shopify)
- Transaction fee (own payment processor): 0% on Shopify Payments
- Payment processing: 1.5% + €0.25 (Shopify Payments, EU)
- No listing fees
- EU VAT: shop owner responsible (OSS registration or Shopify Markets handling)
- Traffic: entirely self-generated

## Margin Comparison: €30 Print-on-Demand Item

Assume: €30 sale price, €14 production cost (POD fulfillment), €4 shipping.

| | Etsy | Shopify (Basic) |
|--|------|----------------|
| Sale price | €30.00 | €30.00 |
| Production | -€14.00 | -€14.00 |
| Shipping | -€4.00 | -€4.00 |
| Listing fee (amortized) | -€0.05 | — |
| Transaction fee | -€1.95 | — |
| Payment processing | -€1.50 | -€0.70 |
| Monthly sub (amortized at 50 sales) | — | -€0.54 |
| **Net margin** | **€8.50 (28.3%)** | **€10.76 (35.9%)** |

At 50 sales/month, Shopify generates €113 more margin per month (€2.26 × 50 sales) vs Etsy at equivalent volume. The monthly Shopify subscription (€27) is covered if you sell 12+ items at this margin differential.

**At 20 sales/month:** Shopify saves €45 in fees but costs €27 in subscription. Net savings: €18/month. Marginal advantage.

**At 100 sales/month:** Shopify saves €226 in fees vs €27 subscription. Net savings: €199/month. Clear advantage.

## The Traffic Question

The fee comparison only matters if you can generate equivalent sales on both platforms. This is where most comparisons go wrong: they treat traffic as equal.

**Etsy marketplace traffic:** Etsy has 90+ million active buyers (global). EU buyers searching for print-on-demand products (custom t-shirts, wall art, greeting cards) find Etsy listings through the platform's search. New shops with zero external audience can generate sales from day one if their listings rank.

**Shopify traffic:** Zero organic marketplace traffic. Every visitor must come from SEO, social media, paid ads, email, or influencer partnerships. A Shopify store opened today with no external audience generates zero sales.

**The practical implication:** For sellers without an existing audience or marketing infrastructure, Etsy is not just cheaper — it is operationally viable where Shopify is not. The traffic question supersedes the fee question.

## When to Use Each

**Start on Etsy when:**
- No existing audience or email list
- Testing product-market fit (Etsy's traffic reveals what sells before you build infrastructure)
- EU VAT compliance management is a constraint (Etsy handles B2C VAT collection under OSS)
- Monthly volume is under 50 sales

**Migrate to (or add) Shopify when:**
- Email list of 500+ subscribers with demonstrated conversion
- Social media following driving traffic to a link
- Monthly volume exceeds 75–100 sales (Shopify margin advantage outweighs subscription cost)
- Wanting to run specific EU VAT structures or bulk pricing not available on Etsy

**Run both in parallel when:**
- Using Etsy for discovery (new customers find you via marketplace search)
- Shopify for retention (repeat customers who already know your brand buy direct)
- POD fulfillment syncs to both (Printful, Printify support multi-channel)

## EU-Specific Considerations

**VAT compliance:** Etsy collects and remits VAT on B2C sales to EU consumers automatically. On Shopify, you need either OSS registration or Shopify Markets (additional cost). For solo operators, Etsy's automatic handling removes a significant compliance overhead.

**GDPR customer data:** Etsy retains customer data and you have limited CRM access. Shopify gives you full customer data ownership — email addresses, purchase history, segmentation access. For building repeat customer relationships, Shopify's data access is materially more valuable.

**Return processing:** EU consumer law (14-day right of withdrawal) applies on both platforms. Etsy's dispute resolution system can disadvantage sellers on customized items. Shopify gives more direct control over return policies, though enforcement remains your responsibility.

The fee structure matters at volume. The traffic question matters at launch. Know which stage you're in.
